“RUSSIAN LANGUAGE IN THE MULTILINGUAL WORLD”
First call


Dear colleagues,
we are happy to announce that the regular section “Russian language in the multilingual world” as part of the XIX April Conference at NRU HSE (National Research University Higher School of Economics) will be held on 10-13th of April 2018.

The section is focused around the following general topics:
  • Corpus research on non-standard Russian
  • Learners corpora: building, compiling, developing and tagging
  • Russian language in bilingual/multilingual environment
  • Heritage Russian
  • Sociolinguistic research in Russian language acquisition
  • Teaching Russian as a foreign language
  • Corpus research in theoretical linguistic

We kindly ask you to present short abstracts of your talks by 29th of January. Abstracts will consist 500 words description of your talk and key words. The information about speaker will include the following information: name and surname, country, affiliation, e-mail.

The conference is organized by the School of Linguistics of the NRU HSE (Moscow). The committee: E.V. Rakhilina, M.A. Krongauz, A.S. Vyrenkova (Marushkina), O.A. Kultepina.

If you have any questions about submitting abstracts or the conference in general, please contact us: rusmultilingworld_hse@yahoo.com.
You can also lead to our conference-page: http://web-corpora.net/RLC/conference

We will be glad to see you at our conference!

Школа Лингвистики НИУ ВШЭ 2017

Смирнов Иван
